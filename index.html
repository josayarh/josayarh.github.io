About Me

Hi I'm a software engineering graduate with experience in game development and R&D. I've studied at the Institut Supérieur d'Informatique, de Modélisation et de leurs Applications in Clermont Ferrand with several stays abroad, 1 year in Canada, 5 months in Ireland, 2 months in Brazil, either as part of my studies or for internships.

During my studies I have specialized myself in Video game development, mostly with the Unity engine but I've also used Unreal and Godot to a lesser degree. Development in Unity means I've become quite proficient with C# but I keep a particular fondness for C++ and I try to keep myself up to date with the updates to the language even though I don't practice it as often as I used too.

I am off course a Video game fan, mostly rpgs and action games nowadays, and I'm very active in some gaming communities. I am an admin and moderator on several large discord servers the largest counting 1000 members and I also help moderate several twitch and youtube channels with livestreams that can range between 50 to 100 consecutive viewers.

Skills

I've been regularly working with Unity for the past 4 years and getting better at it all the time. All the projects above where developed with this game engine.

I have worked extensively with C++ throughout my studies and I try to keep myself up to date on the language with most notably the cppcon conferences.
